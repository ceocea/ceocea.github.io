About

How to play

  1. Click "Sign in through Steam".
  2. Setup your trade URL in your "Account Settings" when you are logged in.
  3. Go back to "Play" and click "Deposit now!" to send the bot a trade offer containing skins you want to deposit.
  4. Once 50 or more skins are deposited, the winner will be randomly chosen.

We take no fee on pots with low value.
After every bet the timer will be reset to 100 seconds.

Trade hold

Once Steam Guard Mobile is activated, you need to wait for 7 days after activation before the trade restriction is removed.

Rules

Please read the following, before you enter in a round:

  • The maximum number of items per deposit is 15. You can deposit as many times as you want per round. The minimum deposit is $0.15.
  • The game ends after 100 Seconds have passed or when 50+ items in total have been deposited.
  • The time it takes to add your deposit to the game varies from 2 to 30 seconds. In rare cases it may take more time because there is a large number of players in queue or Steam is experiencing difficulties.
  • Trades are sent to the winner immediately after the round ends. If you have not received the trade with your prize within five minutes, try out these steps: 1) You have entered the correct trade URL. 2) Your inventory is public (not marked as "private" in your privacy settings). 3) You have less than the maximum of 1,000 items in your inventory.
  • If you do not accept the trade offer within one hour, it will be canceled. This is necessary to keep the bots inventories clean.
  • If you didn't set your trade URL or entered an incorrect one, we can't send you the items! So please make sure the trade URL is correct.
  • Only items for CS:GO are accepted.
  • We take a 3% fee on pots worth $3.00 or more and a 5% fee on pots worth $25.00 or more.
  • We reserve the right to send the winner a prize containing random items with total item value equivalent to the amount won if the initial trade could not be sent or accepted on time.
  • If you have a problem with a trade offer or win, send us a support ticket within 1 hour. When its too late, we might not be able to help you!
  • Each time you visit the website or deposit an item you agree with the rules.

Round Hash
